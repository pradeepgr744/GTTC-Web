About Us

Welcome to Government Tool Room & Training Centre (GTTC)

GTTC is a globally acclaimed Tool Room and Training Centre, established in 1972 as a joint venture of the Government of Karnataka and DANIDA, Government of Denmark. With over 50,000 trained candidates, GTTC has become a hub for skilled manpower and technical services for industries worldwide.

GTTC graduates are highly sought after in countries like Australia, Canada, Singapore, and Malaysia for their expertise in Tool & Die Making, CNC Programming, Mechatronics, and Electronics. Many alumni have also become successful entrepreneurs in precision manufacturing, creating cutting-edge companies and generating employment opportunities.
